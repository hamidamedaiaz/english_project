Electrolysis reaction chamber — Research Lab, Geneva · March 2024

Our proprietary nano-catalyst enables real-time water electrolysis at automotive scale — a breakthrough previously dismissed as thermodynamically impractical. The compact 4-litre reaction chamber integrates seamlessly with existing internal combustion architecture, requiring zero permanent modification to the host vehicle.

By generating hydrogen on-demand at the point of consumption, WATERFLASH eliminates all risks associated with pressurized hydrogen storage — solving the two biggest barriers to hydrogen adoption: safety and infrastructure. No tank. No station. Just water.

Our catalyst operates at ambient temperature and pressure, unlike industrial electrolysis systems. This was made possible by a nano-structured platinum-palladium alloy surface that reduces the activation energy of the splitting reaction by 74%.
